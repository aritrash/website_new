Introducing AstaHome

Your Intelligent Electrical Manager for Ultimate Home Protection and Efficiency

What is AstaHome?

AstaHome is a cutting-edge smart electrical management system designed to safeguard your home and optimize energy usage. It provides comprehensive protection against common electrical faults and intelligently predicts potential issues before they become critical.

Smart Protection, Predictive Power

AstaHome continuously monitors your home's electrical circuits to protect against overvoltage, undervoltage, overload, and short circuits. It acts instantly to mitigate risks, ensuring the safety of your appliances and loved ones.

Beyond traditional protection, AstaHome integrates advanced machine learning algorithms. By performing impedance checking on each connected circuit, it learns electrical patterns and can predict potential short circuits or other anomalies before they occur, giving you proactive alerts.

With AstaHome, you gain not just protection, but also valuable insights into your energy consumption. Its smart features help you identify energy-wasting habits, leading to significant savings on your electricity bills and a reduced carbon footprint.

  • Comprehensive electrical fault protection
  • Machine learning for predictive issue detection
  • Real-time circuit impedance monitoring
  • Energy consumption insights and optimization
  • Seamless integration with smart home ecosystems
