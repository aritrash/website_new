Introducing Thermsilon

Revolutionary Solutions for Smart Water Purification and Thermal Management using Green Energy

What is Thermsilon?

Thermsilon is Asta Epsilon Group's flagship solution for water purification and thermal management in one compact unit, perfect for households with limited space. It combines advanced filtration technology with efficient thermal regulation, ensuring clean water and optimal temperature control for various applications.

Water, like no other!

Instant hot, refreshingly cold, and everything in between. Get water at any temperature you need, from steaming hot for tea, to warm for baby food, or perfectly chilled for a cold drink. This isn't just a water dispenser; it's a smart hydration hub.

Enjoy crystal-clear water with our advanced multi-phase purification: Sediment, Carbon, RO, UV, Mineraliser and UF. Plus, with intelligent auto-maintenance and self-flushing, you can set it and forget it. Pure, perfectly tempered water, effortlessly.

What truly sets our water dispenser apart is its advanced smart functionality, powered by an embedded processor module and smart application integration. This intelligent core brings you unprecedented convenience and peace of mind!

Experience the epitome of hydration technology: compact, cost-effective, pure, and so intelligent, it practically takes care of itself. Elevate your home or office with a water dispenser that truly understands your needs.

  • Reduced energy consumption at 520 watts
  • Improved lifespan of 15 years
  • Minimized operational costs
  • Contributes to lower carbon emissions

Key Features of Thermsilon

Immersion Heating Technology

Uses 300W immersion heater to heat water up to 98°C, ensuring rapid and efficient heating.

Thermo-Electric Cooling

Zero moving parts, zero refrigerants, zero maintenance.

Solar-Powered Electronics

Uses solar energy to power sensors and processor, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources.

Advanced Machine Learning

Automatically learns and adapts to user preferences for optimal performance and energy efficiency.

6 Temperature Presets

Chilled, Cold, Ambient, Lukewarm, Delicate Brews and Instant Ramen presets for versatile usage.

Smart App Integration

Smart app control for remote operation, scheduling, and monitoring. Compatible with iOS and Android devices.
