At Asta Epsilon Group, we are more than just an engineering firm; we are pioneers dedicated to building a sustainable future. Our innovative solutions in smart water management (Thermsilon) and intelligent electrical systems (AstaHome) are designed to make a tangible difference in people's daily lives and the environment.

Our Products: Transforming Lives

Thermsilon revolutionizes home hydration by providing pure, temperature-controlled water on demand, all while being energy-efficient. It simplifies daily routines, enhances health, and reduces household energy consumption, contributing to a greener planet.

AstaHome offers unparalleled peace of mind through its smart electrical management. By predicting and preventing electrical faults like short circuits and protecting against voltage fluctuations, it safeguards homes, extends appliance lifespans, and empowers users with insights into their energy usage, leading to safer, smarter, and more sustainable living spaces.

Joining Asta Epsilon means contributing directly to these impactful innovations, working on technologies that truly improve quality of life and foster environmental responsibility.
